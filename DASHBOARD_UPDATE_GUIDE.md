## üîß Troubleshooting

### Problem: Data Not Updating
**Solution:** Wait 2-3 minutes, then hard-refresh your browser (Ctrl+F5 or Cmd+Shift+R)

### Problem: Old Timestamps Showing
**Solution:** Clear browser cache and refresh the dashboard

### Problem: AI Not Responding
**Solution:** Send the command again or try: "Update the stock dashboard now"

### Problem: Missing Data Sections
**Solution:** Wait for the full 2-3 minute update cycle to complete
